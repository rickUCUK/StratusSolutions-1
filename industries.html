Industries We Serve

Strategic sourcing for every sector. Tailored solutions, delivered with speed and compliance.

Public Sector & Government

Stratus Solutions is a trusted partner to local authorities, government departments, NHS trusts, and public bodies. We respond to open tenders via Contracts Finder and CCS frameworks, providing fully traceable procurement of technology, medical equipment, office furniture, infrastructure goods, and more.

Healthcare & NHS

From PPE and hospital beds to clinical-grade tablets and diagnostics equipment, we support frontline services with critical medical supply procurement. Our team ensures that all products meet ISO and regulatory standards for use in NHS settings.

Education & Research

We provide tailored procurement services for universities, academies, and research institutions. From high-volume computing hardware and scientific lab gear to AV systems and learning technology—we help education buyers meet tight budget and timing requirements.

Energy, Utilities & Infrastructure

Our industrial sourcing team works with utility companies, oil and gas service providers, and infrastructure firms to source specialty parts, chemicals, lubricants, safety gear, and repair kits—backed by on-site delivery coordination and compliance assurance.

Manufacturing & Industrial

We supply UK manufacturers and engineering firms with raw materials, precision tools, MRO (Maintenance, Repair, Operations) products, and process-critical supplies. Whether single purchase orders or framework contracts—we streamline sourcing across the production lifecycle.

Transport, Logistics & Emergency Services

Stratus works with fleet operators, local councils, and emergency response teams to provide transport hardware, traffic management technology, emergency stockpiles, and protective infrastructure equipment under urgent delivery timelines.

Non-Profits, NGOs & Charities

We support mission-driven organisations with socially responsible sourcing and procurement support. Whether you’re responding to a humanitarian request or setting up a new facility, we offer flexibility, fast response, and ethical supply chains.
