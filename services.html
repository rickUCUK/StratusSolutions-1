Our Services

End-to-end sourcing and supply chain services tailored for public and private sector procurement

What We Do

Stratus Solutions is a trusted procurement and sourcing partner to government agencies, NHS trusts, councils, and commercial businesses across the UK. We provide a broad spectrum of solutions—from tech and infrastructure to specialty chemicals and emergency equipment—delivered quickly and in full compliance with regulatory standards.

Technology & Equipment Procurement

We supply laptops, tablets, enterprise hardware, telecommunications gear, and peripherals for educational bodies, healthcare trusts, and central government. Whether it's 1,500 iPads for the NHS or ruggedized tablets for field operations, we handle the entire sourcing lifecycle.

Industrial, Chemical & Oil-Based Sourcing

Our specialist procurement services cover industrial lubricants, lab-grade chemicals, technical oils, and bulk consumables. All supplies are verified, certified, and sourced via vetted UK and international suppliers in line with framework requirements.

PPE & Medical Supplies

From gloves and respirators to defibrillators and sanitisation systems, we supply high-quality, fully compliant PPE and clinical equipment. We also support emergency stockpiling, disaster response, and hospital infrastructure needs.

Public Sector Tender Fulfilment

We work closely with portals like Contracts Finder and Crown Commercial Service (CCS) to respond to tenders quickly and effectively. Our tender specialists identify opportunities, match requirements with supplier capabilities, and ensure fast, traceable fulfilment.

Multi-Category Sourcing Solutions

We manage complex, multi-category requests—whether it’s a single contract covering IT, construction materials, cleaning products, and HVAC units, or category-specific frameworks. With one point of contact, our clients simplify procurement without sacrificing accountability.

Contract Management & Reporting

We provide full reporting and transparency throughout the procurement lifecycle, with compliance documentation, digital tracking, and supplier performance reviews. Our contract support helps clients meet auditing, governance, and SLA compliance targets.
